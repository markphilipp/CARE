CARE's mission is to educate the community about proper pet care and training; decrease the amount of strays and owner surrendered pets; provide veterinary care & shelter for animals, including those who are considered "undesirable" or "non-adoptable." While most groups focus on dogs and/or cats solely, CARE casts a wider net and helps all species of domestic & exotic pets.

Cullen's Archangel RescuE, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) Public Charity
